# KAI ADS v1.0 — SUPREME CONSTITUTION

**Status: SUPREME — v1.0 (ratified from CEO Draft v0.1)**

This is the highest-level document in the entire KAI Ads project.

Not a sprint. Not a backlog. Not a feature list. A constitution.

Every feature, prompt, model change, UI decision, and engineering task must be
evaluated against this document. If a proposed change does not move KAI closer
to these principles, it must not be implemented. Everything built after this
document exists must satisfy it.

Precedence: this document sits ABOVE every other governing document, including
`docs/000_PRODUCT_CONSTITUTION.md` and
`docs/008_ADVERTISEMENT_COMPOSITION_CONSTITUTION.md`. Where any document,
prompt, code comment, or prior decision conflicts with this Constitution, this
Constitution wins. Subordinate documents remain in force for everything they
cover that this document does not contradict.

Amendments: only the product owner may amend this file, and only by editing
this file directly. No code, prompt, or sprint may weaken, reinterpret, or
route around it.

---

## Vision

> Build the world's best AI Recruitment Advertisement Operating System.

Not an image generator.
Not Canva.
Not a GPT wrapper.
Not a poster maker.

A complete Recruitment Creative Operating System.

---

## Mission

A recruitment agency should never leave KAI to create a recruitment
advertisement.

If they compare:

* GPT Pro
* Midjourney
* Canva AI
* Adobe Firefly
* Microsoft Designer

they should still choose KAI — because KAI understands recruitment.

---

## Success Criteria

Every advertisement generated by KAI must be immediately publishable.

* No manual editing.
* No spelling correction.
* No redesign.
* No Photoshop.
* No Canva.
* No GPT refinement.

---

## Golden Rule

**GPT is the Creative Artist. KAI is the Creative Director.**

Never reverse these roles.

---

## Principle 1 — Truth First

Everything generated must originate from verified recruitment information.

* No hallucinated salary.
* No fake benefits.
* No invented client logos.
* No imaginary locations.

---

## Principle 2 — GPT Must Create the Entire Advertisement

* Never rebuild.
* Never redraw.
* Never reposition.
* Never reconstruct.
* Never crop.
* Never regenerate sections.

GPT produces the finished artwork. KAI only validates and enriches it.

---

## Principle 3 — Creative Director Brain

Before GPT receives anything, KAI must think like:

* Creative Director
* Art Director
* Marketing Director
* Recruitment Expert
* Employer Branding Consultant
* Human Psychology Expert
* Newspaper Editor
* Magazine Designer

---

## Principle 4 — Advertisement Must Sell

The purpose is not beauty. The purpose is response.

Every advertisement must maximize:

* Applications
* Calls
* WhatsApp messages
* CV submissions

---

## Principle 5 — Human Psychology

Every advertisement should use:

* visual hierarchy
* attention flow
* eye tracking
* emotion
* urgency
* trust
* scarcity
* authority
* readability

---

## Principle 6 — Commercial Design

Output must equal premium agency quality.

* Never AI-looking.
* Never cartoonish.
* Never clip-art.
* Never template-looking.
* Never amateur.

Must resemble work from a senior commercial designer.

---

## Principle 7 — Photography

Every human must appear:

* photorealistic
* industry accurate
* culturally correct
* PPE compliant
* anatomically correct
* emotionally believable

No plastic skin. No AI faces. No extra fingers. No unrealistic lighting.

---

## Principle 8 — Typography

Typography must equal magazine publishing quality.

Automatically correct:

* spelling
* grammar
* punctuation
* capitalization

Never render obvious spelling mistakes.

(Read with Principle 1 and Principle 14: presentation-level correction only —
spelling, grammar, punctuation, capitalization — never a change to factual
meaning. A corrected word must still refer to the same fact the source stated.)

---

## Principle 9 — Layout

* Whitespace.
* Balance.
* Grid.
* Hierarchy.
* Contrast.
* Alignment.
* Readability.

Never clutter.

---

## Principle 10 — Brand Intelligence

Every agency automatically receives:

* logo placement
* brand colors
* typography consistency
* trust elements
* employer branding

---

## Principle 11 — Trust Layer

Every advertisement contains:

* agency verification
* QR verification
* invisible metadata
* generation ID
* authenticity proof
* subtle agency watermark/fingerprint

The watermark must protect ownership without distracting from the design.

---

## Principle 12 — GPT-Level User Experience

The composer must feel like ChatGPT.

Support simultaneously:

* typing
* drag & drop
* multiple PDFs
* multiple images
* Word documents
* copy/paste
* continued typing after attachments
* attachment removal
* previews

No modal workflow. No forced steps.

---

## Principle 13 — Zero Friction

User journey:

Upload → Type optional instructions → Generate → Publish

Everything else happens automatically.

---

## Principle 14 — Self-Healing Intelligence

Automatically:

* fix spelling
* improve formatting
* detect duplicate information
* normalize currencies
* normalize salaries
* normalize dates
* normalize phone numbers

**without changing factual meaning.**

---

## Principle 15 — Enterprise Engineering

Production standards:

* zero dead code
* zero obsolete pipelines
* zero unused APIs
* zero placeholder docs
* CI/CD
* monitoring
* automated testing
* rollback
* feature flags
* audit logs

---

## Principle 16 — Continuous Benchmarking

Every generated advertisement is automatically benchmarked against:

* GPT Pro
* previous KAI output

KAI must continually improve based on measurable quality, not subjective
opinions.

---
