## Would I deliver V12 as final artwork for ₹5,000?

**NO**

### The 3 biggest remaining creative weaknesses

1. **Benefits are thin and undersell the offer.** "Basic Salary" is a
   non-benefit; two lonely chips look sparse next to benchmarks stacking food,
   accommodation, transport, medical, overtime. Kills desire at the close.
2. **The color story isn't unified.** The cool green/olive sky fights the warm
   gold, muddying the premium identity behind the headline.
3. **No dominant hook — it opens with a brand, not a reason.** Leads with
   "BILFINGER" + a generic line; never lands one specific scroll-stopping
   promise. It informs; it doesn't seduce.

> These weaknesses are exactly what the Creative Brain (see `01-...md`) fixes by
> demoting the employer and elevating destination + earning.
