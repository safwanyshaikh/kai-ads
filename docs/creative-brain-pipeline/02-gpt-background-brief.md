## B. The prompt (copy-paste)

> Cinematic photorealistic photograph, 1:1 square. A single male industrial
> worker seen from behind and slightly to the side, standing on an elevated
> platform, gazing out over a vast oil & gas refinery at golden hour. He wears
> clean high-visibility workwear and a **red hard hat**, shoulders relaxed and
> confident — a quiet, aspirational moment of a skilled tradesman surveying a
> major project.
>
> He is positioned in the **right third** of the frame, his gaze directed
> left-to-right into the plant. Behind him a large petrochemical refinery
> recedes into warm haze: distillation towers, stacks, pipe racks and
> structural steel, with small warm safety lights glowing across the
> structures. A Gulf/desert atmosphere — dry, dusty, immense.
>
> **Lighting:** low golden-hour sun, warm rim light on the worker's shoulders
> and hard hat, long soft shadows, atmospheric backlit haze. **Palette:**
> committed desert gold and warm bronze — amber sky, honeyed light, deep warm
> shadows. Rich, premium, filmic. Full-frame camera, ~50mm, shallow-to-medium
> depth of field, fine grain, high dynamic range, sharp on the worker and
> softening into the haze.
>
> Mood: dignified, cinematic, aspirational — the scale and prestige of Gulf oil
> & gas work. Editorial recruitment-poster quality.
>
> **Composition reserve:** keep the upper-left sky and the lower portion of the
> frame visually quiet so the image reads well with graphic overlay added
> later. Concentrate detail in the mid-right (refinery) and right (worker).
