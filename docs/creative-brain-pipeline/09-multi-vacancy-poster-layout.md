# Multi-Vacancy Poster Layout (LOCKED template)

The approved layout for **multi-vacancy files** (many positions grouped under
many divisions) — e.g. the Halliburton Saudi Arabia poster. This is a **premium
full-page colour poster**, NOT a classified box. Portrait, ~1366×2048 (2:3),
scales to social + newspaper PDF. Generic + data-driven (tenant supplies logos,
client, positions, contact).

## Architecture (top → bottom)

1. **Brand bar (top)**
   - Client logo top-left on its brand-colour block (e.g. Halliburton red).
   - Agency logo top-right (full colour) + agency tagline.

2. **Hero zone**
   - Left: headline stack — eyebrow "URGENT REQUIREMENT" (dark), **COUNTRY** huge
     in the country's premium colour (Country Intelligence) + **flag chip**, then
     a dark **personality/industry badge** ("MAJOR OIL & GAS PROJECT"), then a
     one-line intro sentence.
   - Right: **photographic hero** (GPT background per Visual Story + Industry) —
     e.g. worker in client-branded uniform at a refinery, golden hour. Bleeds to
     the top-right corner.

3. **Positions zone**
   - Red banner "POSITIONS AVAILABLE" with arrow/chevron end.
   - **Multi-column grid of numbered division cards** (4–5 columns). Each card:
     a **numbered circle** (red) + **DIVISION NAME** (red, bold) as header, then
     its **job titles** as `▸` bullet rows on a light-grey card background.
     Divisions flow across columns; long divisions (e.g. Wireline, Crew Basis)
     may span a wider block. Every grounded title listed — grouped, never random.

4. **How-to-apply zone**
   - Icon row: "SHARE YOUR PROFILE WITH: Position Applied For · Expected Salary ·
     Current Location" · "ATTACH YOUR UPDATED CV (Word .doc/.docx only)".
   - **Yellow highlight bar**: "EMAIL SUBJECT MUST INCLUDE: POSITION APPLIED FOR |
     EXPECTED SALARY | CURRENT LOCATION" + an **Example** line (uses the correct
     currency from Currency Intelligence, e.g. `SAR 12,000`).
   - Warning line: applications without correct subject/format may not be considered.

5. **Trust footer (bottom)**
   - Client name block (brand colour, left) · agency logo · email · **MEA LICENSE
     NO.** + RA number · **SINCE <year>** · QR verification.

## Colour system
- Driven by **Country Intelligence premium colour** + client brand colour.
- White / very-light body; one or two accent colours max (client red + country
  gold/blue); yellow reserved for the apply highlight. High contrast, no clutter.

## Typography (KAI 5-font set)
- Eyebrow + country headline + division numbers: **Anton / Archivo Black** (hero).
- Banners, card headers, labels: **Oswald**.
- Job-title lists: **Barlow Condensed** (dense, readable).
- Salary/example numerals: **Archivo Black**.
- Intro + footer/legal: **Roboto Condensed**.

## Highlights / devices (locked)
- Numbered red circles per division.
- Red "POSITIONS AVAILABLE" chevron banner.
- Dark personality badge under the country headline.
- Country flag chip beside the headline.
- Yellow email-subject highlight with worked Example.
- Client brand-colour footer bar + MEA licence + SINCE year + QR.

## Rules
- Positions **grouped by division** (Position Intelligence), all grounded titles
  shown; Level I/II/III kept as-is unless the tenant asks to merge.
- Currency in any salary/example must match the country (Currency Intelligence).
- Hero photo matches Industry + Visual Story; client-branded uniform when the
  client logo is supplied.
- No dead space; premium, mobile-readable; one hero only.
- All logos are supplied assets — never scraped/recreated.

> This layout is the default for **multi-vacancy** requirements. Single-role or
> salary-table requirements may use the classified box styles (see doc 07).
