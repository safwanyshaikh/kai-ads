# KAI ADS

## Product Constitution

Version: 1.0

Status: LOCKED

Owner: KAI Platform

Last Updated: July 2026

---

# Mission

KAI Ads enables licensed recruitment agencies to create professional overseas recruitment advertisements in less than 60 seconds using AI.

The platform is built specifically for overseas recruitment agencies and is not a generic AI image generator.

---

# Vision

Become the global operating system for recruitment advertisements by combining artificial intelligence, recruitment expertise and structured recruitment intelligence.

Every advertisement generated today becomes knowledge for KAI tomorrow.

---

# Core Philosophy

Less typing.

More selecting.

Maximum automation.

Every screen must save recruiter time.

---

# Golden Rules

Rule 1

Never ask users to type information that AI can extract.

---

Rule 2

Never ask users to repeat information already known.

---

Rule 3

Every feature must reduce recruiter workload.

---

Rule 4

AI assists.

Recruiters approve.

---

Rule 5

Generated advertisements must always remain editable.

---

Rule 6

Only regenerate the edited section.

Never regenerate the whole advertisement.

---

Rule 7

The application is designed for recruiters.

Not designers.

---

Rule 8

Every advertisement becomes structured recruitment intelligence.

---

# Product Scope

KAI Ads creates recruitment advertisements.

It does not replace ATS.

It does not replace CRM.

It does not replace Canva.

It replaces repetitive advertisement creation.

---

# Users

Primary Users

• Licensed Overseas Recruitment Agencies

Future Users

• Domestic Agencies

• Employers

• Government Skill Centres

# Product Objectives

Reduce advertisement creation time.

Improve advertisement quality.

Information Required

# Trust

# Editing

Every advertisement is divided into editable blocks.

Header

Industry

Requirement

Benefits

Interview

Footer

Editing one block must never affect other blocks.

---

# Learning

Every advertisement contributes anonymous structured recruitment intelligence.

This data improves future KAI products.

---

# Product Principle

The user should spend time recruiting people.

Never designing advertisements.
